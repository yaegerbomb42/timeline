# User Guide - Timeline Improvements

## What's New

Your Timeline app just got a major upgrade! Here's what's changed and how to use the new features.

## 🎨 Visual Emotional Journey

### Sentiment-Based Color Gradient

The timeline rollercoaster now changes color based on your emotional state:

- **🔴 Red**: Negative emotions, sadness, difficult times
- **🟡 Yellow/Orange**: Neutral, balanced states
- **🟢 Green**: Positive emotions, happiness, good times

**How to Use:**
- Just look at your timeline - the colors show your emotional journey at a glance
- Red sections show challenging periods
- Green sections show happier times
- Smooth color transitions show how your mood evolved

**Example:**
```
Day 1-3: Deep red     → You were going through a tough time
Day 4-5: Yellow       → Things stabilized
Day 6-10: Bright green → Great period of positivity!
```

## 🤖 Intelligent Mood Analysis

### Gemini AI-Powered Insights

Every entry now receives deep analysis from Google's Gemini AI:

**What You Get:**
1. **Detailed Rationale**: 4-5 sentence analysis explaining:
   - Your emotional patterns
   - Consciousness level
   - Underlying themes
   - Contextual understanding

2. **Consciousness Assessment**: Quick summary like:
   - "observant text" - You were focused on external observations
   - "self discovery" - You were introspecting and learning about yourself
   - "overthinking reality" - You were philosophically ruminating
   - "social connection" - You were experiencing relationships
   - "hopeful" - You had an optimistic outlook

**How to Access:**
- **Single Click** on any timeline node → Opens the entry
- **Double Click** on any timeline node → Opens detailed mood analysis modal
- Hover over nodes to see quick summary

**What's Analyzed:**
- Emotional tone and sentiment
- Language patterns and word choice
- Self-awareness and introspection depth
- Context: life events, relationships, growth
- Consciousness indicators and thought patterns

### Automatic Processing

**No Action Required!**
- Existing entries are automatically analyzed in the background
- New entries are analyzed immediately when you create them
- You'll see a processing indicator if analysis is in progress

**How It Works:**
1. The app checks for entries without AI analysis
2. Processes them in batches of 15
3. Waits 3 seconds between batches (to respect API limits)
4. Updates your timeline automatically

**Check Progress:**
- Look for the mood analysis status indicator
- Shows: "Processing X of Y entries"
- Will complete in the background even if you close the app

## 🗺️ Enhanced Timeline Overview

### Minimap Improvements

The minimap at the bottom now gives you better navigation:

**What Changed:**
- **Bigger and Clearer**: Height increased for better visibility
- **Better Density Bars**: Easier to see which days have more entries
- **Improved Handles**: Easier to grab and resize the view window

### Dynamic Range Display

**New Feature: See What You're Viewing**

The minimap now shows exactly what portion of your timeline is visible:

**Examples:**
- "Viewing: 1 day" - Very zoomed in
- "Viewing: 10 days" - Zoomed in on a specific period
- "Viewing: ~2 weeks" - Medium zoom
- "Viewing: ~3 months" - Zoomed out
- "Viewing: ~2.5 years" - Very zoomed out
- "Viewing: Entire timeline" - Seeing everything

**How to Use:**
1. **Click** anywhere on minimap → Jump to that point
2. **Drag** the view window → Pan through your timeline
3. **Resize** from left or right edge → Zoom in/out
4. The text updates to show current view range

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### "Processing..." Takes Long?
- The app processes 15 entries at a time
- Large timelines (hundreds of entries) take longer
- It will complete in the background
- You can keep using the app while it processes

### Colors Not Showing?
- Colors are based on mood ratings
- New entries might need a moment to process
- Refresh the page if colors seem stuck

### Minimap Not Responding?
- Make sure you're clicking inside the minimap container
- Try dragging from the center of the view window
- Resize handles are at the left and right edges

### Analysis Modal Won't Open?
- Try double-clicking directly on the node (the circle)
- Make sure the entry has been processed (not still pending)
- Check that the entry has mood analysis data
